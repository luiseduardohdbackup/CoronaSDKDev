= Splorr!! Wrath of the Dagger =

== The Story ==

Welcome to the world of Splorr!!

In Wrath of the Dagger, you play a young person coming of age.  

The ritual of adulthood in your village consists of being thrown down a pit into a maze of rooms filled with monsters, traps, and hidden doors.

== The Goal ==

If you survive and find a way out, then you will be accepted into the ranks of adulthood.

If you don't, then you're dead.

== Getting Started ==

You start with nothing.
